Andres Jimenez Larios

The colorful rugby-playing twin

No matter how many hits I take, I get back up

I am originally from the Inland Empire (a region just outside of Los Angeles) but I moved up to the Bay Area for college. Although the COVID-19 pandemic hit and made me move around a lot, I have learned how to make the best of the situations I am in. Here is both the fun and professional side of me!

Here is me traveling back from Mexico after vacationing with family. I also took the time to look at airplanes, which I love a lot.

I am a rugby player and I have been playing for about 2 years now. I have played for the San Francisco Fog team. Rugby has taught me a lot about teamwork, discipline, and resilience. It has also given me the opportunity to travel and meet people from all over the world.

I am also a multimedia reporter and I have been working on my Master's Degree in journalism about the Bay Area for the past two years. I have been taking photos of the people, places, and events that make the Bay Area unique. I have also been writing articles to accompany my photos with Bay City News and Local News Matters . I hope to continue working on this project and to share it with others.
